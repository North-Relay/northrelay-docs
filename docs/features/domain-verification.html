Domain Verification

How to verify your custom sending domain for email authentication and improved deliverability

Domain Verification

Verifying your custom domain allows you to send email from your own addresses (e.g., notifications@yourdomain.com) instead of a NorthRelay system subdomain. Verified domains get proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC alignment, which significantly improves deliverability and brand trust.

Prerequisites

  • Pro tier or higher ($19/mo) -- custom domains are not available on Free, Starter, or Micro plans
  • Access to your domain's DNS management (e.g., Cloudflare, Route 53, GoDaddy)
  • A domain you own and control

Free, Starter, and Micro tier users send from NorthRelay system subdomains on north-pipe.ca. To use your own domain, upgrade to Pro or higher.

Step-by-Step Verification

Step 1: Add Your Domain

  1. Go to Dashboard > Domains
  2. Click Add Domain
  3. Enter your domain hostname (e.g., mail.yourdomain.com or yourdomain.com)
  4. Click Add

NorthRelay will generate the DNS records you need to add.

Step 2: Get Your DNS Records

After adding the domain, the dashboard shows three DNS records to configure:

SPF Record (TXT)

Authorizes NorthRelay's mail servers to send on behalf of your domain.

FieldValue
TypeTXT
Host@ or yourdomain.com
Valuev=spf1 include:_spf.north-pipe.ca -all

If you already have an SPF record, add include:_spf.north-pipe.ca before the -all or ~all mechanism:

DKIM Record (CNAME)

Points to NorthRelay's DKIM signing key for your domain.

FieldValue
TypeCNAME
Hostdefault._domainkey
ValueProvided in the dashboard (unique per domain)

Verification Record (TXT)

A one-time record that proves you own the domain.

FieldValue
TypeTXT
Host_northrelay-verify
ValueProvided in the dashboard (unique per domain)

Step 3: Add Records to Your DNS Provider

Add all three records through your DNS provider's management interface. Common providers:

  • Cloudflare: DNS > Add Record (set Proxy Status to "DNS only" for CNAME)
  • Route 53: Hosted Zones > your domain > Create Record
  • GoDaddy: DNS Management > Add Record
  • Namecheap: Advanced DNS > Add New Record

DNS propagation can take up to 48 hours, though most changes take effect within 15 minutes to 2 hours.

Step 4: Verify

  1. Return to Dashboard > Domains
  2. Click Verify next to your domain
  3. NorthRelay checks for all three DNS records
  4. On success, the domain status changes to Verified

Managing Domains

Viewing Domain Status

Go to Dashboard > Domains to see all your domains with their current verification status, DNS record status, and last verification check time.

Re-verifying a Domain

If DNS records change or verification expires, click Re-verify on the domain's row in the dashboard. NorthRelay will re-check all DNS records.

Removing a Domain

  1. Go to Dashboard > Domains
  2. Click the domain you want to remove
  3. Click Delete Domain

Removing a domain immediately stops email sending from that domain. Any emails in queue with that domain's from address will fail.

Troubleshooting

Verification Fails: SPF Record Not Found

Cause: The SPF TXT record has not propagated or contains a syntax error.

Fix:

  1. Check that the TXT record for your domain contains include:_spf.north-pipe.ca
  2. Verify there is only one SPF record (multiple SPF records cause failures)
  3. Use the Pre-Flight Check tool to validate your DNS
  4. Wait for DNS propagation (up to 48 hours) and retry

Verification Fails: DKIM Record Not Found

Cause: The CNAME record for default._domainkey is missing or pointing to the wrong value.

Fix:

  1. Confirm the CNAME host is exactly default._domainkey.yourdomain.com
  2. If using Cloudflare, ensure the proxy is disabled (DNS only / gray cloud)
  3. Verify the CNAME target matches the value shown in your dashboard

Verification Fails: Verification TXT Not Found

Cause: The _northrelay-verify TXT record is missing or has the wrong value.

Fix:

  1. Check the record host is _northrelay-verify.yourdomain.com
  2. Confirm the value matches exactly (no extra spaces or quotes)
  3. Some DNS providers add the domain suffix automatically -- enter just _northrelay-verify as the host

DNS Propagation Delays

DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate globally. If verification fails immediately after adding records:

  1. Wait 15-30 minutes and try again
  2. Use a DNS lookup tool (e.g., dig or a web-based checker) to confirm records are visible
  3. If records are visible in DNS but verification still fails, contact support
