Email Delivery Failure Diagnosis

When emails are not being delivered, work through these steps in order:

WireGuard Tunnel Down

Symptoms

  • Emails queue up but never deliver
  • Ping to 10.0.1.2 or 10.0.2.2 fails
  • wg show shows no recent handshake

Step 1: Check Tunnel Status

Look for:

  • latest handshake: Should be within the last 2 minutes. If blank or old, the tunnel is down.
  • transfer: Should show non-zero bytes. Zero rx bytes means no traffic is coming back.

DKIM Signing Failures

Symptoms

  • Outbound emails arrive without a DKIM-Signature header
  • DMARC alignment fails
  • Email clients show "not authenticated" warnings

Step 1: Check OpenDKIM on the Relay VPS

Step 2: Verify Key Files Exist

Step 3: Verify KeyTable and SigningTable

Step 4: Verify DNS Record

Step 5: Restart OpenDKIM

Note: Remember that NorthRelay uses OpenDKIM on the relay VPSes, not Stalwart's built-in DKIM. Stalwart's DKIM signing is disabled (queue.outbound.sign.enable = false). Always check the relay VPS, not the Sanctuary VPS.
