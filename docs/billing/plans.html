Sandbox (Free)

The Sandbox tier is for testing and evaluation. No credit card required.

  • 3,000 emails per month -- enough to test your integration
  • Shared IP pool -- your emails share an IP with other free-tier senders
  • Sends from a NorthRelay system subdomain (yourname.north-pipe.ca)
  • Basic inbox access with 100 MB storage
  • Full API access with 5 templates and 1 contact list
  • No custom domains, webhooks, or team features

Best for: Developers evaluating NorthRelay, personal projects, proof-of-concept integrations.

Micro ($5/month)

The Micro tier provides a cleaner sending environment at a low cost. It acts as the gateway tier -- better than free, with a clear upgrade path.

  • 10,000 emails per month
  • Shared IP pool -- same pool as Sandbox but with better rate limits
  • Sends from a NorthRelay system subdomain (no custom domains)
  • Inbox access with 500 MB storage
  • 25 templates and 5 contact lists
  • No custom domains, webhooks, or team features

Best for: Small projects, low-volume transactional email, developers who need more than the free tier.

Startup ($29/month)

The Startup tier is the first tier with full feature access. It unlocks custom domains and the isolated IP pool, which is the core NorthRelay differentiator.

  • 50,000 emails per month
  • Isolated IP pool -- your emails route through a physically separate IP, protected from other senders' reputation
  • Custom domains -- send from your own domain with full SPF/DKIM/DMARC alignment
  • Webhooks -- receive real-time delivery notifications
  • Inbox access with 5 GB storage
  • Unlimited templates and contact lists
  • Full diagnostic tools access

Best for: SaaS products, growing businesses, anyone who needs deliverability guarantees.

Scale ($65/month)

The Scale tier adds higher volume and team collaboration.

  • 150,000 emails per month
  • Isolated IP pool -- same protected infrastructure as Startup
  • Custom domains with full authentication
  • Webhooks for delivery tracking
  • Team members -- invite colleagues with role-based access
  • Inbox access with 25 GB storage
  • Unlimited templates and contact lists
  • Full diagnostic tools access

Best for: High-volume senders, marketing teams, multi-person operations.

Enterprise (Custom Pricing)

The Enterprise tier provides dedicated infrastructure and white-glove support.

  • 200,000+ emails per month (custom limits)
  • Dedicated IP pool -- your own IP addresses, not shared with any other sender
  • Dedicated IPs -- one or more IPs assigned exclusively to your account
  • Custom domains with full authentication
  • Webhooks and team members
  • Inbox access with 50 GB storage
  • Priority support with guaranteed response times
  • Custom SLA tailored to your requirements
  • Custom onboarding and migration assistance

Best for: Large organizations, high-volume marketing platforms, businesses with strict compliance requirements.

Contact sales for Enterprise pricing.

Understanding IP Pools

NorthRelay's core differentiator is physical IP separation between tiers. This is the Two-Pool System.

Shared Pool (Sandbox + Micro)

  • Emails route through a shared public IP
  • IP reputation is influenced by all senders on the pool
  • Vulnerable to the "noisy neighbor" problem -- if another sender on the pool behaves badly, it can affect your deliverability
  • HELO hostname: shared.north-pipe.ca

Isolated Pool (Startup + Scale)

  • Emails route through a separate, dedicated IP via WireGuard tunnel
  • IP reputation is isolated from the shared pool
  • Protected from noisy neighbors
  • Consistently higher deliverability
  • HELO hostname: sovereign.north-pipe.ca

Dedicated Pool (Enterprise)

  • Your own IP addresses, used only by your account
  • Complete control over IP reputation
  • Custom HELO hostname available

For a deeper explanation, see the Two-Pool System documentation.

How to Upgrade

  1. Go to Dashboard > Billing
  2. Click Change Plan
  3. Select your new tier
  4. Enter payment information (if not already on file)
  5. Confirm the upgrade

Upgrades take effect immediately. You get instant access to the new tier's features and sending limits. See the Billing FAQ for proration details.

How to Downgrade

  1. Go to Dashboard > Billing
  2. Click Change Plan
  3. Select a lower tier
  4. Confirm the downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You keep your current tier's features until then. Note that downgrading may disable features (e.g., custom domains, webhooks) that are not available on the lower tier.
