Billing FAQ

Frequently asked questions about NorthRelay billing, plan changes, and payments

Billing FAQ

Plan Changes

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Dashboard > Billing > Change Plan.

  • Upgrades take effect immediately. You get instant access to the new tier's features and sending limits.
  • Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You keep your current features until then.

Is my billing prorated when I upgrade?

Yes. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you are charged the prorated difference for the remaining days in your billing period. For example, if you upgrade from Micro ($5/mo) to Pro ($19/mo) halfway through your cycle, you pay approximately $7 for the remaining half-month.

Is my billing prorated when I downgrade?

No. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You are not refunded for the remaining days on your current plan, but you retain full access to your current tier's features until the cycle ends.

What happens to my custom domains if I downgrade below Pro?

Custom domains are a Pro+ feature. If you downgrade to Micro or Free:

  • Your custom domains are deactivated (not deleted) at the end of the billing cycle
  • Emails will send from your NorthRelay system subdomain instead
  • Your domain configuration is preserved -- if you upgrade again, your domains reactivate without needing to re-verify DNS

What happens to my webhooks if I downgrade below Pro?

Webhooks are a Pro+ feature. If you downgrade:

  • Webhook endpoints are paused at the end of the billing cycle
  • No events are delivered while paused
  • Webhook configurations are preserved -- upgrading reactivates them

What happens to my team members if I downgrade below Scale?

Team members are a Scale+ feature. If you downgrade:

  • Team member access is revoked at the end of the billing cycle
  • Team member accounts are preserved -- upgrading restores their access

Quotas & Limits

What happens if I exceed my monthly email quota?

When you reach your monthly sending limit:

  • Additional send requests return an error: 451 4.7.1 Rate limit exceeded
  • The API returns a QuotaExceededError with a clear message
  • Already-queued and in-progress emails continue to deliver
  • Your dashboard shows a warning when you reach 80% of your quota
  • Your quota resets at the start of your next billing cycle

You can upgrade your plan at any time to get a higher quota immediately.

Do unused emails roll over to the next month?

No. Email quotas reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused emails do not carry over.

Is there an overage option instead of upgrading?

Not currently. If you regularly exceed your quota, upgrading to the next tier is the recommended approach. Enterprise plans can include custom quota arrangements -- contact sales.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

NorthRelay accepts Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit and debit cards. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

What currency are charges in?

All charges are in USD (US Dollars).

When am I charged?

You are charged on the same day each month, based on the date you first subscribed. For example, if you signed up on March 15, you are charged on the 15th of each month.

What if my payment fails?

If a payment fails:

  1. You receive an email notification
  2. Stripe automatically retries after 3 days, then again after 5 days
  3. If all retries fail, your account is downgraded to the Free tier
  4. You can resolve it at any time by updating your payment method and retrying

No data is lost during a payment failure. Your account, domains, templates, and contacts are all preserved.

Can I pay annually?

Annual billing is not currently available for self-service plans. Enterprise customers can arrange annual billing -- contact sales.

Refunds

What is your refund policy?

NorthRelay offers a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first payment for any paid plan. If you are not satisfied within 14 days of your first charge, contact support for a full refund.

After the 14-day window:

  • Monthly charges are not refundable once the billing cycle has started
  • Downgrades take effect at the end of the cycle (no partial refund)
  • If there is a service issue on NorthRelay's end (outage, billing error), refunds or credits are handled on a case-by-case basis

How do I request a refund?

Email support@northrelay.ca with your account email and the reason for your request. Refund requests within the 14-day window are processed within 5-10 business days.

Free Tier

What are the Free tier limitations?

The Free tier includes:

  • 3,000 emails per month
  • Shared IP pool (shared with other free and Micro tier senders)
  • Sends from a NorthRelay system subdomain only (no custom domains)
  • 100 MB inbox storage
  • 5 templates, 1 contact list
  • No webhooks or team features

There is no time limit on the Free tier. You can use it indefinitely.

Do I need a credit card for the free tier?

No. The Free tier requires no credit card and no payment information.

Enterprise

How does Enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise pricing is custom based on your requirements:

  • Volume: Monthly sending volume above 200,000 emails
  • Infrastructure: Dedicated IPs, custom HELO hostnames
  • Support: Priority support with guaranteed response times, dedicated account manager
  • Compliance: Custom SLA, data processing agreements, security reviews

Contact sales for a quote.

Can I get a dedicated IP on a non-Enterprise plan?

Dedicated IPs are only available on the Enterprise tier. Pro and Scale tiers use the isolated pool, which provides strong reputation protection without a dedicated IP. For most senders, the isolated pool delivers equivalent results.

Do you offer non-profit or startup discounts?

Contact sales@northrelay.ca to discuss special pricing for qualified non-profits and early-stage startups.
