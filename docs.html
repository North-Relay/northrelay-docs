Why NorthRelay?

The "Noisy Neighbor" Problem

When you use traditional email services, you share IP addresses with thousands of other senders. If one of them sends spam, your emails suffer too.

The NorthRelay Solution

Our Two-Pool System gives you control:

  • Shared Pool - For development and low-volume senders (free & $5/mo)
  • Isolated Pool - Dedicated reputation for production apps ($29+/mo)
